About Us

Our Story

Founded in 2014, VS Facility Management Services was built on a simple mission: to deliver exceptional housekeeping and facility management solutions to the institutions that matter most – hospitals, schools, and colleges.
What began as a small team of dedicated professionals has grown into a comprehensive facility management company, proudly serving 50+ institutions across the region. Our unwavering commitment to excellence, safety, and reliability has made us the trusted partner for managing critical environments.
The company was founded by Mr. Amit Jadhav, who brings over 20 years of experience in the hospitality industry, having worked with some of the most prestigious properties. His leadership continues to inspire our focus on quality and customer satisfaction.
Today, VS Facility Management Services continues to innovate, expand, and adapt—while maintaining the personal touch and attention to detail that our clients have always valued.

From emergency services to clean energy solutions, we deliver results that matter to communities and businesses alike.

Waste Management

Facility Management

At VS Facility Management Services, we recognize that modern facilities require more than just routine manual cleaning to maintain the highest standards of hygiene and efficiency. With the increasing size and complexity of institutions such as hospitals, educational campuses, malls, and corporate offices, manual cleaning alone often falls short in delivering consistent and comprehensive results. To address this, we have integrated mechanized cleaning into our facility management operations, ensuring that every corner is cleaned with precision and effectiveness.

Our services include the use of advanced equipment such as high-performance scrubber dryers, industrial vacuum cleaners, ride-on sweepers, and pressure washers. These machines are designed to handle large areas in significantly less time while providing a deeper and more uniform level of cleanliness. By using mechanized cleaning, we minimize human error, reduce the risk of cross-contamination, and ensure that hygiene standards remain consistently high, especially in sensitive environments such as hospitals where patient safety is a priority.

The adoption of cleaning machines not only enhances the quality of our services but also improves overall efficiency and sustainability. Mechanized processes reduce water and chemical usage, lower labor fatigue, and increase productivity, allowing our staff to focus on more detail-oriented tasks. This approach aligns with our commitment to safety, reliability, and innovation, enabling us to provide clients with solutions that are both effective and environmentally responsible. Through this integration of technology and expertise, we continue to raise the benchmark for facility management services.
